Designer Story

Prof. Jimmy Choo OBE

Cambridge, United Kingdom, Feb 2, 2018 —— “Gems of the Silk Road by Professor Jimmy Choo, OBE” is opened to the public ceremoniously in the Cambridge International Art Centre – R.G. Hung’s Gallery today showcasing the pieces created with the crossover collaboration between the internationally celebrated shoe designer Prof. Jimmy Choo OBE and talented new designer Reggie Hung.

Reggie Hung, MA, Designer

The two designers have made a bold attempt to introduce their new jeweled shoe brand as a crossover collaboration to emphasize their exquisite techniques and artistic ingenuity and try to have combine those two concepts to pass on the traditional classic craftsmanship to future generations in an innovative way.
