Gems of the Silk Road by Professor Jimmy Choo, OBE

Reggie Hung &
Prof. Jimmy Choo OBE

The exhibition “Gems of the Silk Road by Professor Jimmy Choo, OBE” will be unveiled in the Cambridge International Art Centre – R.G. Hung’s Gallery at 2 pm on Feb 2, 2018. It will open to the public for one month until March 2.

The exhibition “Gems of the Silk Road
Cambridge International Art Centre – R.G. Hung’s Gallery at 2 pm on Feb 2, 2018.

The exhibition “Gems of the Silk Road by Professor Jimmy Choo, OBE” will be unveiled in the Cambridge International Art Centre – R.G. Hung’s Gallery at 2 pm on Feb 2, 2018. It will open to the public for one month until March 2.

With its theme of “Peace and Kindness”, the exhibition will showcase unique pieces created with the crossover collaboration between the internationally celebrated shoe designer Mr. Jimmy Choo and talented new designer Reggie Hung. A collection of jewelry designed by Reggie Hung, inspired by the last pair of shoes made by Mr. Jimmy Choo for Princess Diana, will be displayed. A pair of jeweled wedding shoes, which is the latest piece designed by Mr. Jimmy Choo will be exhibited at the same time. Sets of jeweled mini-shoes will also be on display. Each piece implies beautiful meanings, and contains the unique understanding of designers on the collaboration of culture, art and jewelry.
