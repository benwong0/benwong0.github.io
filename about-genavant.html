Brand Story

The second generation of the shoemaker family business, Prof. Jimmy Choo OBE and the second generation of jewelry design family business, Reggie Hung make a bold attempt to introduce their new jeweled shoe brand as a crossover collaboration. Since both shoes and jewelries emphasize the exquisite techniques and artistic ingenuity, they try to have combine those two objects to pass on the traditional craftsmanship to the next generation in an innovation way.

This latest luxury brand Genavant Prof. Jimmy Choo launched is originated from the name of a goddess in one of his favorite Malaysian songs since he was a child.

Our Products
& Brands

This latest luxury brand Genavant Prof. Jimmy Choo launched is originated from the name of a goddess in one of his favorite Malaysian songs since he was a child.

In Prof. Jimmy Choo’s mind, the brand will be like the goddess of fashion, passing on the spirit of love ---- the family love, friendship, and the longing for love, symbolizing that Genavant will shine on the people as the goddess of love. It also will be another form of inheritance for his craftsmanship.

The new brand will mainly target on individual customization, and will have more made-to-order shoes for celebrities and royals, therefore those delicately crafted shoes inlaid with jewelries will be worn by more people, with its unique gleam.
